Smartwatch

This innovative smartwatch combines a gyroscope and a responsive touch interface, enabling advanced motion tracking and user interaction. With its ESP32 microcontroller and Wi-Fi connectivity, it provides seamless data synchronization and communication with other devices, making it a versatile tool for fitness tracking and daily activities.

Automated Blinds

Controls blinds using an ESP32 and NEMA 23 stepper motor with a planetary gearbox, opening at 6 AM on weekdays and 9 AM on weekends, closing at 10 PM, based on real-time synchronization.

Ticker

This ESP32-based ticker retrieves real-time stock and cryptocurrency data from an API and displays it on a connected screen, allowing for seamless monitoring of market trends and price movements.

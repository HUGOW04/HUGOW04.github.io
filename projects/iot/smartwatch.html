Smartwatch with Gyroscope, ESP32, Touch, and Wi-Fi

Project Overview

This smartwatch integrates a gyroscope, an ESP32 microcontroller, a touch interface, and Wi-Fi connectivity. It's designed to provide a variety of functionalities, including motion tracking, wireless communication, and user interaction through a responsive touch screen.

Capabilities

The smartwatch can measure and interpret rotational movement using the integrated gyroscope, making it ideal for fitness tracking and activity monitoring. The ESP32 microcontroller handles sensor data and supports Wi-Fi connectivity for data synchronization and remote communication with other devices.

The touch interface allows users to navigate and interact seamlessly, providing an intuitive experience. This smartwatch is a versatile device suitable for a wide range of applications, enabling users to stay connected and informed.
